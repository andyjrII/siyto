Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to questions you might have

Anyone with a viable hustle or business idea can apply – including market traders, artisans, shop owners, students, and startup founders. We especially support women and youth-led businesses.

We offer:
- Small Business Loans
- Starter Loans (for first-time borrowers)
- Women & Youth Empowerment Loans
- Community Lending (for cooperatives or groups)

You can apply online through our website or visit our office. You'll need to fill out a short application form and provide some basic documents.

We review applications within 48 hours. If approved, your funds will be disbursed promptly – often within 1 to 3 working days.

You'll need to provide:
- Valid ID and passport photo (for both applicant and guarantor)
- Guarantor details
- Proof of business or explanation of your idea
- Bank statement or transaction history

Not always. Some loan types may require a guarantor or small form of security. We assess this on a case-by-case basis.

Our interest rate is a flat 10%. Repayment plans are customized based on the loan amount and your business cycle. We'll explain everything clearly before you commit.
